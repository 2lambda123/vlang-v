Usage: 
  v [flags] doc module_name output_file[.json|.md|.html]
  v [flags] doc path_to_source.v output_file[.json|.md|.html]
  v [flags] doc ./directory output_file[.json|.md|.html]
  v [flags] doc . output_file[.json|.md|.html]

Generates the documentation of a given directory, module, or V source file 
and prints or saves them to its desired format. It can generate HTML, JSON, 
or Markdown format.

Options:
  -pub_only  Documents only the publicly listed functions/methods/structs/enums. 
             Defaults to "true".
